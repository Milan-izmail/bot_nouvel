# Product Bot Rules

## Nouvel Amour Flowers

Nouvel Amour Flowers is a premium floral salon in Kyiv. The brand sells not only flowers, but emotion, a gesture of attention, service, aesthetics, and a moment the recipient remembers.

This is not Nouvel Amour Aroma. Do not use Aroma, aromas, candles, diffusers, IFRA, wax, room sprays, or body sprays.

## SalesBox

SalesBox needs a short, practical product description for managers. It should quickly explain what the product is, who it suits, what occasion it fits, and why it feels like a thoughtful gift.

SalesBox text should be compact, clear, and useful in conversation with a client.

## ShopExpress

ShopExpress needs a complete ecommerce product card: short description, full description, composition, recipient, occasion, impression, SEO fields, categories, tags, attributes, alt texts, and substitution note.

ShopExpress text should be publication-ready.

## Short Description vs Full Description

Short description:

- 2 to 3 concise sentences.
- Explains emotion, format, and use case.
- Works in SalesBox and the upper product-card area.

Full description:

- 2 to 4 short paragraphs.
- Explains what the product is, for whom, for what occasion, what feeling it creates, and why it is a premium gift.
- Does not only describe the photo.

## Bouquet Rules

For bouquets, describe the gesture, occasion, recipient, emotional effect, color mood, and presentation.

Do not invent flower composition or stem count unless provided or confidently visible.

## Box Composition Rules

For floral compositions in boxes, explain why the format is convenient and gift-ready: easy to present, easy to transport, already arranged, good for interiors.

Mention color palette, scale, occasion, and premium salon presentation.

## Mono Bouquet Rules

For mono bouquets, focus on restraint, flower type, color, rhythm, and clarity of the gesture.

Do not overdecorate the text. Mono bouquets should feel intentional, not plain.

## Add-On Product Rules

For toys, balloons, greeting cards, plants, and add-on gifts, write as complementary products. Explain how they support the main gift.

Do not add the flower substitution note unless the product includes flowers.

## SEO Rules

Every product card must include:

- seo_title, up to 60 characters when possible.
- meta_description, up to 155 characters when possible.
- h1.
- slug in lowercase Latin letters, digits, and hyphens.

Use natural keywords: delivery flowers Kyiv, buy bouquet Kyiv, flowers in box, premium bouquet, author bouquet, birthday flowers.

Do not keyword-stuff.

## Alt Text Rules

Alt texts should describe the product and help SEO. Use 3 to 5 alt texts.

Do not name exact flowers if the composition is not known.

## Substitution Note

For every bouquet and floral composition, include exactly:

У разі відсутності деяких квітів вони замінюються на аналогічні або дорожчі за рахунок компанії зі збереженням кольорової гами, стилю та ключових характеристик букета або композиції.

## Composition Rules

Do not invent exact flower composition. If unknown, use:

Склад уточнюється флористом. Композиція може бути адаптована відповідно до наявності квітів зі збереженням кольорової гами, стилю та загального враження.

## What To Avoid

Do not only describe the photo.

Do not use generic phrases.

Do not use long dash characters.

Do not use bold or italic Markdown.

Do not publish weak text.
